Solar System

Our solar system is home to the Sun and everything that orbits it. It includes eight planets: Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune. These planets vary widely in size, composition, and atmosphere. The Sun, a medium-sized star, dominates with its immense gravity, holding the planets and other celestial bodies in orbit. Moons, asteroids, comets, and dwarf planets like Pluto also populate our solar system, each contributing to its rich and diverse cosmic tapestry
